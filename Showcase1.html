A CURATED PLAYLIST

CELEBRATING KANYE WEST'S CONTRIBUTION TO MUSIC, FASHION, AND ART.

Looking for an excuse to collaborate with long time friend and uber talented illustrator
Anu Chouhan, These Beats are Dope is a curated playlist celebrating Kanye West's
contribution to music, fashion, and art. A combination of Spine and the web APIs from
Spotify and Youtube, the project launched with a feature on One Page Love, an honorable
mention from Awwwwards, and much love from the music community.
